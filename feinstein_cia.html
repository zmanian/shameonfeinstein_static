Shame on Feinstein Coalition Applauds Senator Feinstein's stand against unconstitutional domestic surveillance of the Senate Select Committee.

On March 11, Senator Feinstein took to the floor of the Senate to denounce CIA surveillance of the Senate Select Committee's investigation of torture in the CIA's Rendition, Detention and Interrogation program.

The techniques used against the Senate Committee by the CIA are applied to every American by the National Security Administration (NSA). We applaud Senator Feinstein's recognition that these activities are unconstitutional and troubling to the majority of Americans.

Senator Feinstein's strong objections to CIA monitoring of the Senate Select Intelligence Committee reflect the international conversation that has occurred since June of 2013 regarding blanket surveillance by US government agencies and the inability by legislative oversight bodies to effectively oversee surveillance activities.

The Bay Area-based Shame on Feinstein Coalition (www.shameonfeinstein.org) hopes the Senator will, in light of this new information, reconsider some of her previous positions on NSA mass blanket surveillance.

Zaki Manian of Shame on Feinstein notes "a culture of deception and lawlessness has run rampant in the United States' intelligence agencies during Dianne Feinstein's tenure as Chair of the Select Committee. All unconstitutional surveillance must end immediately".
